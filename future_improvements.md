# Future Improvements - Kingdom Wars

## Strategic Gameplay Enhancement
1. **Real-time Strategy Elements**: Transition from turn-based to hybrid real-time/turn-based gameplay
2. **Diplomacy System**: Complex diplomatic relationships with trade agreements, alliances, and betrayals
3. **Multi-layered Combat**: Land, sea, and air warfare with different unit types and strategies
4. **Siege Warfare**: Detailed fortress siege mechanics with siege engines, walls, and defensive structures
5. **Resource Economics**: Complex economy with multiple resource types, trade routes, and market fluctuations

## Kingdom Management
6. **City Building**: Detailed city construction with districts, infrastructure, and population management
7. **Technology Trees**: Research systems unlocking new units, buildings, and strategic options
8. **Cultural Development**: Kingdom culture affecting unit morale, research speed, and diplomatic options
9. **Nobility System**: Manage lords, generals, and advisors with personal relationships and ambitions
10. **Succession Planning**: Dynasty management with heirs, marriages, and political implications

## Advanced Warfare
11. **Tactical Battle Maps**: Detailed battlefield terrain affecting unit movement and combat effectiveness  
12. **Unit Customization**: Design custom army compositions with specialized roles and equipment
13. **Weather & Seasons**: Environmental effects on movement, supply lines, and battle outcomes
14. **Espionage Network**: Spy systems for intelligence gathering, sabotage, and political manipulation
15. **Mercenary Companies**: Hire specialized mercenary units with unique abilities and loyalty mechanics

## Multiplayer & Social
16. **AI Campaign System**: Persistent AI worlds with dynamic alliances and long-term strategic challenges
17. **AI Faction Systems**: Complex AI alliances with shared territories, resources, and coordinated warfare
18. **Historical Campaigns**: Recreate famous historical battles and campaigns with accurate units and tactics
19. **Scenario Editor**: Tools for players to create custom maps, campaigns, and victory conditions
20. **Achievement System**: Ranked challenges with seasonal goals and personal achievement tracking
